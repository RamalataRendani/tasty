Events

Private Dining at Tasty

Tasty Private Dining Room is an elegant and intimate space that can seat up to 22 guests for dinner daily. The centerpiece of the room is a single, stately table crafted by Maine artisan Greg Lipton. Set beneath a wood-beamed ceiling and rustic chandeliers by Celeste Gainey, the table is complemented by early American antiques and art adorning the walls. Our exclusive florist, Roberta Bendavid, is available to create custom seasonal florals to put the finishing touches on your table. For groups of a larger size, we can also offer semi-private options and full restaurant buyouts, custom pricing is available to fit the occasion
