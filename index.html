Welcome to Tasty Restaurant

The Restaurant

One of South Africa's most beloved restaurants,Tasty is a place to see and be seen and have warm hospitality, and unparalled service for over two decades.Chef Michael Anthony's ever-evolving seasonal menu showcases the restaurant's relationships with local farms and purveyors.

The restaurant has erned eight awards, including "Outstanding Restaurant" and "Outstanding Chef in South Africa".
